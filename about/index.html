hi :)

I'm Paolo Lunardon, an italian interaction designer based in Milan, currently working @Bending Spoons.

With a strong focus on functionality, I love designing digital experiences that are not only visually appealing, but functional and intuitive to use, simplifying the interaction between people and technology!

I'm a ‘Precisino’

My friends call me 'precisino' (the precise one) because I'm a very meticulous person always paying attention to every detail, whether it's in a design project or in my everyday life.

“I love to solve real problems”

I love functional design and, as designer, I want to make things easier for people. I like to immerse myself in usability research and prototyping to create truly practical, user-centred solutions.

A full-time Nerd

In my spare time, I'm a nerd who loves to always dive into new passions. Whether it's a software, a new sports or hobby, I like to spend hours watching tutorials, reading forums and experimenting things trying to master some new skills!

You can find me playing piano on my Youtube channel, baking a good loaf of sourdough bread (with my “Bovi” Sourdough) or doing some calisthenics handstand and pull-ups.
